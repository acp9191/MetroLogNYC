# Privacy Policy

**MetroLog NYC**

*Last updated: January 2025*

## Overview

MetroLog NYC is a subway station tracking app that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device only**:
- Your visited station history
- Your app preferences

No data is transmitted to external servers. No account or sign-up is required.

## Location Data

The app may request access to your location to show your position on the map. This location data:
- Is used only while the app is open
- Is never stored or recorded
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Can be disabled in your device Settings

## Analytics & Tracking

This app does **not** use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not collect:
- Usage statistics
- Device information
- Personal identifiers
- Crash reports

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
